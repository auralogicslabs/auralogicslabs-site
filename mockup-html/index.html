FAQ's

Here is a list of frequently asked questions about Auralogics Labs and the Nexora suite.

What integrations are available with Auralogics?

Nexora connects with WordPress, Search Console, PageSpeed Insights, WP-CLI, webhook endpoints, and existing SEO plugins while keeping your data under your control.

Can I add multiple users or agencies to my account?

Yes. The portal model supports teams, permissions, audit logs, and agency workflows.

How does Nexora secure the web performance layer?

HMAC-signed capture requests, Ghost Protocol fingerprint stripping, and rollback-safe static publication reduce operational risk.

How can I control user permissions within my account?

Portal roles let teams separate site operators, billing contacts, support agents, and administrators.
